## Design Decisions
- Qibla window: R*0.182 wide, R*0.117 high, positioned at cx+R*0.48, cy+R*0.015
- Qibla alignment: 5° tolerance, green glow + pulse + audio chime
- Chime volume: 0.075 base × tickVol slider
- Tick volume: quartz 1.0, mechanical 0.60 base × tickVol slider
- iOS requires click (not touchstart) for DeviceOrientation permission
- AudioContext shared between qibla chime and tick system
- City coords fallback for Qibla (35+ cities hardcoded)
- calcQibla() called at init + after prayer data loads + after activateAll

## Sound Design Journey
- Quartz: pure noise impulse, fast decay (600), no resonance — "dry tick"
- Mechanical: damped sinusoids (2200Hz + 5800Hz) + noise transient
- Tried oscillator-based ticks — Tawfeeq preferred noise burst approach
- Pre-generated buffers caused different sound — reverted to fresh per-tick
- Volume went through many iterations: started at 0.08, ended at 1.0/0.60 with slider
