## 11. 💭 Concepts

**AGOT → Prix Ars Electronica "Digital Humanity"**
The app is literally digital humanity: a Muslim prayer clock built by an African immigrant at ILM, using WebGL + GPS + gyro + spatial audio + generative Islamic geometry, free, during Ramadan, for a global Muslim community. It's not a concept project — it's live, it's real, it shipped during Ramadan. That's the submission.

**fatiha.app → Grand Prix submission next year**
Too early for this year's deadline, but the CONCEPT (voice recitation → procedural calligraphy) is exactly what Prix Ars Electronica Digital Humanity and S+T+ARTS want. Begin documenting the project narrative now, even before MVP. Documentation IS submission.

**Autoresearch loop → fatiha.app stroke model**
Karpathy's pattern: small GPU, tight training loop, overnight experiments. Our target: stroke sequence model for Arabic calligraphy. Training data exists (KHATT, IFN/ENIT, AHDB). A6000s can handle it. The autoresearch pattern is how we find the best architecture without manual tuning.

**Make Noise NUSS × Ableton Link Audio × TouchDesigner**
Multichannel modular audio → Link Audio streams into Ableton → MIDI output triggers TouchDesigner visuals → Each audio channel maps to a visual parameter. Spatial audio rig + synchronized AV = performance piece that's all interconnected.

**DLA from cube base → AGOT visual experiment**
Fractal organic growth radiating from the glass cube's contact point with the floor. Grows slowly over the minute/hour, resets. A living floor texture rooted in physics. Tentative: "the cube grows roots." Distinctly anti-decorative — grounded, structural.
