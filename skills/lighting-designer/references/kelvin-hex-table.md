## Common Pairings for Warm/Cool Contrast

| Warm (Key) | Cool (Fill) | Mood |
|-----------|------------|------|
| 2700K + 7500K | Cozy interior with window light | Residential night |
| 3200K + 6500K | Tungsten vs daylight | Classic cinema |
| 4000K + 8000K | Neutral vs shade | Modern dramatic |
| 5500K + 2700K | Sun vs incandescent | Mixed lighting |
