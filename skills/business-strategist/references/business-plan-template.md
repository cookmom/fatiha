# Business Plan Template

Use this structure when creating a complete business plan. Fill each section with specifics — no placeholders or vague language.

## Product
- **What**: One-sentence description of the product/service
- **For whom**: Specific target user/customer
- **Problem it solves**: The pain point, quantified if possible
- **Unique value**: Why this over alternatives
- **Status**: concept / building / MVP / launched / scaling

## Business Model
- **Revenue type**: donations / subscriptions / one-time / freemium / licensing
- **Pricing**: Specific tiers and amounts
- **Revenue split** (if applicable): Partner/platform percentages
- **Cost structure**: hosting, domain, APIs, tools, time — estimated monthly

## Market
- **Target audience**: Demographics, psychographics, behaviors
- **Market size**: TAM / SAM / SOM with sources
- **Competitors / alternatives**: Named competitors with positioning
- **Our edge**: Specific differentiation

## Go-To-Market Strategy
- **Phase 1 — Seed**: Validate demand, first 100 users
- **Phase 2 — Grow**: Scale acquisition, hit product-market fit signals
- **Phase 3 — Scale**: Expand channels, markets, or product lines
- **Key channels**: Ranked by expected CAC efficiency
- **Partnerships**: Named potential partners and structure
- **Launch date / deadline**: Specific timeline

## Marketing
- **Brand / positioning**: One-line positioning statement
- **Domain / URL**: Actual or planned
- **Social presence**: Platforms and strategy
- **Content strategy**: Topics, cadence, format
- **Key messages**: 3 core messages for target audience

## Follow-Up Strategy
- **User retention**: Specific mechanisms (email, features, community)
- **Feedback loops**: How you'll collect and act on feedback
- **Update cadence**: Release/content schedule
- **Community building**: Strategy and platforms
- **Re-engagement**: Seasonal, lifecycle, or trigger-based

## Evangelists / Partnerships
- **Who promotes this**: Profile of ideal evangelist/partner
- **Application / onboarding process**: Steps and timeline
- **Incentive structure**: Compensation, recognition, access
- **Screening / vetting**: Criteria and process
- **Dashboard / tools for evangelists**: What they get
- **Legal / liability**: Agreement structure and protections

## Resources
- **Team**: Roles and current capacity
- **Tools / stack**: Technology and services
- **Budget**: Monthly burn and runway
- **External help needed**: Gaps to fill

## Tracking & Metrics
- **KPIs**: 3-5 primary metrics with targets
- **Analytics**: Tools and dashboards
- **Milestones**: Time-bound goals for next 3-6 months

## Decision Log
Track key decisions and rationale as the plan evolves.

## Open Questions
List unresolved items that need research or decisions.
