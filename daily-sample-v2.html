💡 Concept of the Day

The Callback Machine

Islamic geometric patterns are generated by one simple rule repeated — a single star, rotated and reflected, fills an infinite plane. The girih (knot) hides its generating seed. What if you built a generative piece that starts with visual chaos, then slowly reveals the single rotation rule underneath? The audience watches order emerge from noise. The reveal: it was always one shape. The callback: now they can't unsee the seed in any pattern they encounter in the real world. A perceptual one-way door — applied to sacred geometry.
