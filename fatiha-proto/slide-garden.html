The garden does not exist before the voice. It is not drawn but grown, each polygon deformed as wet pigment bleeds into damp paper. Eighty-three events of tajweed unfurl along a golden spiral — and from recitation, an Ottoman garden blooms on the screen as it once bloomed in the margins of a manuscript.
