### For Muslim Tech People:
> Assalamu alaikum! I built a free Islamic prayer clock web app called "A Gift of Time" (agiftoftime.app) — it uses Three.js for beautiful 3D visualization with Islamic geometric patterns, calculates prayer times based on your location, and has zero ads or data collection. Launching it as Ramadan begins as a sadaqah jariyah. Would mean a lot if you'd check it out and share with your audience if you think it's worthy. JazakAllah khair! 🕌

### For Tech/Creative Coding People:
> Hey! Built something I think you'd appreciate — a prayer clock web app using Three.js with real-time Islamic geometric patterns and WebGL rendering. The tech: [brief specifics]. It's free and open [if applicable]. Would love your thoughts: agiftoftime.app

### Key Principles:
1. **Keep it short** — Respect their time
2. **Show, don't tell** — Attach a video/GIF, not just a link
3. **Don't ask for shares explicitly** — "Would love your thoughts" > "Please retweet"
4. **Personalize** — Reference their specific work/interests
5. **Follow up once** — If no response in 3-4 days, one gentle follow-up, then move on
