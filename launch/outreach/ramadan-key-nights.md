# Ramadan Content Engine — Key Nights, Jumu'ahs & Milestones

> Every key moment = a themed dial + a post + a deep link + a share moment

## Concept

Create **themed dials** for significant Ramadan nights, Fridays, and milestones. Each dial features a relevant Quranic surah with a listen button. Social posts link directly to that dial — visitors land on the verse and tap to listen.

**URL format:** `agiftoftime.app/#laylatul-qadr` or `agiftoftime.app/?dial=qadr`

**Flow:** Social post → deep link → themed dial → surah audio → share → viral loop

---

## The Three Phases of Ramadan

Ramadan is traditionally divided into three 10-day periods (ashra):

| Phase | Days | Theme | Hadith Basis |
|-------|------|-------|-------------|
| **First Ashra** | 1-10 | **Mercy** (Rahma) | "The first ten days are mercy..." |
| **Second Ashra** | 11-20 | **Forgiveness** (Maghfira) | "...the middle ten days are forgiveness..." |
| **Third Ashra** | 21-30 | **Salvation** (Najat) | "...and the last ten days are freedom from the Fire" |

This gives each content moment a thematic anchor beyond just the date.

## Implementation Requirements

### Deep Link Routing
- URL hash (`#dial-name`) or query param (`?dial=name`) parsed on load
- Routes to specific dial + auto-scrolls to surah section
- If dial has special surah, preload that audio
- Hash options needed: `#ramadan-begins`, `#jummah-1` through `#jummah-4`, `#mid-ramadan`, `#badr`, `#last-ten`, `#night-23`, `#night-25`, `#laylatul-qadr`, `#night-29`, `#eid`

### New Dials / Themes Needed
- **Laylatul Qadr** — deep celestial silver/blue (MUST HAVE)
- **Last 10 Nights** — darker, contemplative variant
- **Badr / Victory** — bold, warm, strength
- **Eid** — sunrise gold, celebration
- **Jummah gold** — warm Friday variant (could be existing dial with gold accent)

### Surah Audio Sources
All via Quranic Audio CDN (Mishary al-Afasy, murattal):
- `https://download.quranicaudio.com/qdc/mishari_al_afasy/murattal/{number}.mp3`
- Al-Kahf: 18 | Ar-Rahman: 55 | Ya-Sin: 36 | Al-Anfal: 8
- Ad-Dukhan: 44 | Al-Muzzammil: 73 | Al-Fajr: 89 | Al-Qadr: 97
- Al-Ikhlas: 112 | Al-A'la: 87

### Content Pipeline Per Moment
1. Draft post 2-3 days ahead
2. Screenshot the themed dial (day + night mode)
3. Prepare social graphic (screenshot + Arabic verse overlay)
4. Schedule or post manually after Maghrib on the target evening
5. Engage with comments for 2-4 hours after posting

## Why This Works

1. **13 content moments** across 30 days — almost every other day without forcing
2. **Deep links = immersive** — Click → land on the verse → listen. Not "download our app"
3. **Spiritually grounded** — Every surah choice has a reason. Knowledgeable Muslims will notice.
4. **Three-phase arc** — Mercy → Forgiveness → Salvation mirrors the Ramadan journey
5. **Jummah rhythm** — Weekly touchpoint that every Muslim already observes
6. **Builds to a crescendo** — Week 4 intensity matches the spiritual intensity of the last 10 nights
7. **Sharable by design** — "Send this to someone" is natural when the content is a Quran verse
8. **Sadaqah jariyah framing** — Every share is ongoing charity, not marketing
9. **Badr anniversary** — Almost nobody does this. It's a differentiator that shows depth.
10. **Eid closer** — Ends on gratitude, not promotion
