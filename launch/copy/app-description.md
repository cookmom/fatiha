## Full Description

**A Gift of Time** is a stunning Islamic prayer clock that runs entirely in your web browser. Built with Three.js by a Senior VP Engineer at Industrial Light & Magic, it brings 20+ years of creative technology expertise to something deeply personal: helping Muslims connect with their daily prayers.

### Features
- **Accurate Prayer Times** — Calculated for your exact location using established Islamic astronomical methods
- **Qibla Compass** — Great-circle bearing to the Kaaba, updating in real-time
- **Spatial Adhan** — Immersive, spatially rendered call to prayer that plays at each prayer time
- **11 Clock Dials** — Handcrafted 3D dial designs, each with its own character and beauty
- **Night Mode** — A soft, dark interface designed for late-night prayers and Ramadan nights
- **Ramadan Fasting** — Suhoor and iftar countdowns with a fasting progress ring
- **Works Everywhere** — Runs in any modern browser on desktop or mobile
- **Offline Support** — PWA-enabled so it works even without a connection
- **No Ads** — Ever
- **No Tracking** — Your location stays on your device
- **No Sign-Up** — Just open it and go
- **Open Source** — Sadaqah jariyah (continuing charity) for the global Muslim community

### The Story Behind It
A Gift of Time was created by Tawfeeq Martin (@LOOKMOM), a Senior VP Engineer at ILM on the StageCraft team (the virtual production technology behind The Mandalorian and other major productions). After 20+ years building creative technology for Hollywood, Tawfeeq wanted to build something for his own community — not for a client, not for a studio, but as a gift.

The result is a prayer clock that's as beautiful as it is useful. It's free, it's open source, and it's intended as sadaqah jariyah — a continuing charity whose rewards, insha'Allah, extend beyond this life.
